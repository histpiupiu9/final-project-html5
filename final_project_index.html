About Michigan Lighthouses

Michigan has more lighthouses than any other U.S. state. They helped ships navigate the Great Lakes for centuries and remain important cultural landmarks today.

Quick facts

  • Michigan has over 120 historic lighthouses.
  • Many lighthouses are open to the public as museums or parks.
  • They are found along Lake Superior, Lake Michigan, Lake Huron, Lake Erie and Lake St. Clair.

Visiting Tips

If you plan to visit lighthouses, follow these guidelines for safety and accessibility:

  1. Check opening times before you go; many are seasonal.
  2. Wear sturdy shoes for rocky or uneven walkways.
  3. Respect signage and barriers: some areas are protected or closed for safety.

Learn more about lighthouses and their history at the U.S. National Park Service: Lighthouses.
